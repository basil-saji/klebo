Workflow Integration
Klebo seamlessly blends into the table by clamping to the edge to liberate valuable workspace, functioning as a stable, integrated extension of the desk that allows for fluid, one-handed access without interrupting your workflow.
Easy Tape Removal
Klebo simplifies refills with a quick-release spool hub that slides out smoothly, allowing for effortless tape changes in seconds.
Exploded View
Clip-on Mechanism
Featuring an under-chassis clamping mechanism and rubber grips, Klebo anchors to the desk edge to ensure stability and seamless tape access during workflow.
Safety Mechanism
Klebo features a retractable blade mechanism that keeps the cutting edge shielded behind the ribbed TPE guard when idle, only exposing it under specific cutting tension to prevent accidental injury.
Extra Storage
The lower chassis features a dedicated compartment to store spare tape rolls for quick access.
Packaging Design Concept
The packaging conveys engineered simplicity and substantial quality through its clean lines, two-tone geometry, and prominent Volkswagen branding.
